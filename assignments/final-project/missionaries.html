Full Time Temple Missionaries

If you've been called to be full-time temple missionaries, congratulations! This is no doubt an exciting opportunity! We want to help in any way we can.

All of our locations offer long-term stay options in one of our suites! The rooms include a king-sized bed, living area, full bath, and a kitchenette. The kitchenette is fully eqquiped with all of the cooking utinsels you could need. We also have suites with a second bedroom for those who may need it.

Pricing varies from location to location. If you're interested in this option, please fill out this contact form below, and a representative will reach out to you within three business days. You can also call 515-928-6791 on weedays to speak to a representative.
