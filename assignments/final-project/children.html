Daycare Services

When there's young children at home, it can sometimes be hard to make it to the temple. Finding a reliable and trustworthy caretaker can be difficult and expensive. That's why Temple Inn & Suites is offer a new, low-cost short term daycare service! Our professional caregivers are all certified to handle any situation. We'll take children 18 months to ten years, for up to six hours Tuesday through Saturday. Prices vary by location, but costs can be as low as $18 a child for the whole six hours! For exact pricing, contact the individual hotel.
