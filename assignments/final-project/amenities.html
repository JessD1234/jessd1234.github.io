Amenities

Here at temple Inn & Suites, we're not just about providing somewhere for temple patrons to stay when they're making overnight trips. We also provide many convenient services for our guests as well as all temple patrons. Here is a list of some of the services you might find useful:

  • Free access to changing rooms and restroom facilities.
  • Short-term babysitting services–for more information, see our Children Acommodations page.
  • A wedding reception hall–for more information see our Reception Rooms page.
  • A family search center with on-site family history experts.
  • Long-term stay accommodations full-time temple missionaries–for more information see our Full Time Missionaries page.

For those who do book a stay with us, all rooms include these benefits:

  • Free Continental Breakfast
  • Indoor Pool
  • High-speed internet access and smart TVs
  • Free Parking
  • Free Temple Shuttle (From 6A.M. to 7P.M. Tuesday–Saturday)
