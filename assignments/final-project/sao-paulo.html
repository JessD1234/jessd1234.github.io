    A Blessing for South America

    The Sao Paulo Brazil temple was the first temple ever dedicated in Brazil, or South America, for that matter. At the time, the church required that members of the surrounding area raise one-third of the cost to build the temple. Many of the saints in Brazil had very little, but they gave their all, knowing the blessings of the temple was worth the sacrifice. Before the building of the Sao Paulo temple, saints in South America had to travel to Washington D.C., where the closest temple was located. That was quite a trip, and many couldn't afford to go. Needless to say, having a temple in their home country was an amazing blessing to the saints in Brazil.

    For a time, the temple was so busy they held sessions through the night between Friday and Saturday to allow more people to attend, since many made the journey to the temple on the weekends.

    It is fitting that this is also our first international location. Come see for yourself what the faith of the South American saints produced!
