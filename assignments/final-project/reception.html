Reception Rooms

Our reception rooms are the perfect place to hold a gathering of any type, but they're especially suited to celebrate the new Mr. and Mrs! With customizable menus and decorations, we have everything you'll need, right next door to your temple of choice.

Our rooms are spacious enough for dinner and dancing, and during the warmer seasons an outdoor patio is available for use. Our staff will handle serving the meals, decorating, and cleaning up afterwards. The rooms are available for reservation for five hour blocks, Monday through Saturday, from 9AM until 10PM. Please schedule your reservation at least three months in advance, but to increase your chances of claiming your preferred time and date, we suggest scheduling up to six months in advance.

To just rent the room and our available furniture, the cost is $600. But, if you're looking for a little more, let us help you make your occasion truly special! Below are the services we offer.
